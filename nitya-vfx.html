How it works

From brief to
picture lock

A simple four-step loop that keeps every shot and every artist in sync through the whole production lifecycle.

1
Create Project
Set up a new production, add a description, and assign your artist roster from the Projects page.
2
Add Shots
Register each shot with a code, frame range, and initial status. Priority slider keeps the most critical work visible.
3
Track Progress
Artists update status and append history entries as work progresses — a live audit trail per shot.
4
Ship It
Mark shots Done, store folder links for final deliverables, and archive the project when picture locks.
